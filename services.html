OCS Layout Design

The success of a contact-line system is critically dependent on its dynamic interaction with the pantograph. With state-of-the-art IT systems and software tools developed in-house – like Sicat Master and Sicat Dynamic – we simulate and analyze the efficiency of the entire system in advance. Connections with reliable, corrosion-resistant, and low-maintenance components ensure the maximum efficiency of contact lines for mass transit and main-line railways.

AC Traction Power Supply

Whether it is high-voltage switchgear, protection technology, or converters, you’re guaranteed consistently outstanding quality with seamlessly coordinated components. You’ll benefit by saving resources in the planning and operation of your AC traction power supply – right from the start.

DC Traction Power Supply
